Algebra

Algebra is a branch of mathematics dealing with symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. It is about finding the unknown or putting real-life variables into equations and then solving them.

Example Problem

Solve for x: 2x + 3 = 7

Solution: x = 2

Geometry

Geometry is a branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, and solids.

Example Problem

Find the area of a triangle with base 5 and height 10.

Solution: Area = 0.5 * base * height = 25

Calculus

Calculus is the mathematical study of continuous change, dealing with derivatives and integrals of functions.

Example Problem

Find the derivative of f(x) = x^2.

Solution: f'(x) = 2x

Statistics

Statistics is the science of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting large quantities of numerical data.

Example Problem

Find the mean of the data set: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10.

Solution: Mean = (2 + 4 + 6 + 8 + 10) / 5 = 6
