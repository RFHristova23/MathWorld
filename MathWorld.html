Algebra

Algebra is a branch of mathematics dealing with symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. It is about finding the unknown or putting real-life variables into equations and then solving them.

Example Problem

Solve for x: 2x + 3 = 7

Solution: x = 2

Geometry

Geometry is the branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, and solids. It deals with the shape, size, and relative position of figures and with properties of space.

Example Problem

Find the area of a circle with radius r.

Solution: Area = πr²

Calculus

Calculus is a branch of mathematics that involves the study of rates of change. It is divided into two main branches: Differential Calculus and Integral Calculus. It has widespread applications in science and engineering.

Example Problem

Differentiate f(x) = x² + 3x + 2.

Solution: f'(x) = 2x + 3

Statistics

Statistics is the study of the collection, analysis, interpretation, presentation, and organization of data. It is used in a wide variety of academic disciplines, from the natural and social sciences to the humanities.

Example Problem

Calculate the mean of the data set: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10.

Solution: Mean = 6
